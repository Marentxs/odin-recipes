How to: T-shirt

Description

A lightweight, breathable, everyday short-sleeve shirt made from cotton or a cotton blend. Ideal for casual wear and layering.

Ingredients

  • 1 to 1.5 yards of cotton jersey knit fabric
  • Matching thread
  • Ribbing for neckline (optional)
  • T-shirt pattern (front, back, sleeve)
  • Fabric scissors
  • Pins or clips
  • Sewing machine or serger

Steps

  1. Cut fabric pieces using the T-shirt pattern: front, back, and sleeves.
  2. Pin and sew the shoulder seams (front to back).
  3. Attach and sew the sleeves to the armholes.
  4. Sew side seams from armpit to hem in one continuous line.
  5. Finish the neckline with ribbing or by folding and topstitching.
  6. Hem the sleeves and bottom using a double-needle or zigzag stitch.
  7. Press all seams and hems for a clean finish.
