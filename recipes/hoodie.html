How to: Hoodie

Description

A warm, soft hooded sweatshirt typically made from cotton fleece. Includes a front pocket and drawstring hood â€” great for casual and active wear.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 2.5 yards cotton/poly fleece
  • Rib knit fabric (for cuffs and waistband)
  • Matching thread
  • Hoodie pattern (body, sleeves, hood, pocket)
  • Drawstring (optional)
  • Sewing tools

Steps

  1. Cut out all pieces using the hoodie pattern (front, back, sleeves, hood, pocket).
  2. Sew shoulder seams of front and back together.
  3. Attach sleeves to the armholes; then sew down the sides.
  4. Assemble and attach the kangaroo pocket to the front piece.
  5. Sew hood pieces together and attach to neckline.
  6. Add ribbed cuffs and waistband by folding rib knit and stitching on.
  7. Insert drawstring into hood (if applicable), then press all seams.
