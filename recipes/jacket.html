How to: Jacket

Description

A mid-weight, structured outerwear piece featuring a front zipper, lining, and optional collar or hood. Designed for layering in cooler weather.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 yards of outer fabric (canvas, nylon, denim, etc.)
  • 1.5-2 yards of lining fabric
  • Separating zipper
  • Matching thread
  • Jacket pattern (outer and lining pieces)
  • Optional: interfacing, elastic, snaps

Steps

  1. Cut all outer and lining fabric pieces using the jacket pattern.
  2. Sew outer shell: attach front, back, sleeves, and collar/hood.
  3. Sew the same for the lining, leaving a small hole in one seam.
  4. Insert lining into the outer shell (right sides together); align edges.
  5. Sew around jacket opening, securing lining and outer fabric.
  6. Turn jacket right side out through the lining hole and close hole by hand or machine.
  7. Insert and topstitch zipper; press all seams and check alignment.
